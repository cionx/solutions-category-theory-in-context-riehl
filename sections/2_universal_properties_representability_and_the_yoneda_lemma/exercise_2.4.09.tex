\subsection{}

We have the following definition of a discrete right fibration:
\begin{quote}
	A functor~$Π \colon \cat{E} \to \cat{B}$ is a \defemph{discrete right fibration} if for every morphism~$f \colon c \to d$ in~$\cat{B}$ and every object~$e$ in the fibre over~$d$ there exists a unique lift of~$f$ along~$Π$ with codomain~$e$.
\end{quote}
